Have you ever been to a coffee farm?

If you have, then you know it is one of the most amazing experiences besides jumping off a plane. Being surrounded by nature's beauty can be breathtaking and humbling. You will get to experience how life at the farm is like and lean one on one with a family that dedicates their life to coffee.

What do I have to do?

All you have to worry about is purchasing your plane ticket and we will take it from there. Whether it is a family trip or a company trip, we will be arranging transport, lodging and nutrition so that all you have to do is indulge in a good time and create lasting memories. As soon as you land in San Pedro Sula we will take you to our house in the city where you can relax and rest while enjoying the view of the city in the front yard or a virgin mountain with a bird sanctuary right behind. If you are lucky, you can even feed the monkeys! The next day we will take you to the farm (1 hour dive) and stay for the night. Depending on the duration of your stay we can go to the beach or the lake and enjoy more of Honduras's hidden gems. We want to make sure that you have the experience of a lifetime. Your enjoyment is our motivation!
